# Spot the Recommendations!

Time to become a recommendation detective. Grab your phone or tablet and let's go hunting.

**Your Mission:** Find 5 different types of recommendations in the wild.

Open YouTube (or TikTok, or whatever you use most). Look at your home screen. Don't tap anything yet -- just LOOK. Can you spot:

1. **A "picked for you" recommendation** -- Something the app chose specifically because of your watch history. How can you tell? It might be related to something you watched recently.

2. **A "trending" recommendation** -- Something popular right now that everyone is seeing, not just you. Look for labels like "Trending" or fire icons.

3. **A "because you watched..." recommendation** -- The app telling you exactly WHY it picked something. Netflix is great at this. YouTube sometimes says "Based on your watch history."

4. **A "people also liked" recommendation** -- Suggestions based on what OTHER people enjoyed. You might see this in app stores or shopping sites.

5. **A totally wrong recommendation** -- Something that makes NO sense for you. Maybe it's in a language you don't speak. Maybe it's about something you've never been interested in.
