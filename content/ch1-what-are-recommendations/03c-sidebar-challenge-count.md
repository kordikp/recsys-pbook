Ready for a challenge? Grab your phone and a timer. We're going to see just how FAST recommendation systems really are.

## Here's What To Do

1. Open YouTube (or TikTok, or any app with a feed)
2. Set a timer for exactly **10 seconds**
3. Scroll through and COUNT every single recommendation you see
4. Stop when the timer goes off

## How Many Did You Get?

Most people count somewhere between **30 and 50 recommendations** in just 10 seconds. That's about 5 per second flying past your eyes!

Now think about this: imagine a librarian trying to suggest 5 books per second. They'd have to grab a book, look at the cover, think about whether you'd like it, hand it to you, and then do it all again -- five times every second. Impossible!

But here's the wild part: for a computer, 5 per second is actually **slow**. YouTube's recommendation system can generate **millions** of personalized recommendations per second, across all its users. It just shows YOU a few at a time because, well, you only have two eyes.

## Bonus Challenge

Try it on three different apps. Which one throws the most recommendations at you? You might be surprised -- some apps are way more aggressive than others.

**The takeaway:** You are swimming in recommendations all day long. Most of the time, you don't even notice them. Now you will.
