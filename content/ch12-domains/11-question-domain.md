You've explored recommendation challenges across multiple domains. Which one resonates most with your work or interests?

---

**A) Video & Streaming**
You work on content discovery for a streaming platform, or you're fascinated by how Netflix and YouTube decide what to show next.

---

**B) E-Commerce**
You're focused on conversion optimization, product discovery, and the direct revenue impact of recommendations.

---

**C) News & Media**
You care about information quality, editorial independence, and the societal implications of algorithmic curation.

---

**D) Music & Podcasts**
You're interested in mood-aware, context-dependent recommendations and the unique challenge of playlist curation.

---

**E) Marketplaces or Specialized Domains**
You work with unique items, two-sided matching, geographic constraints, or other domain-specific challenges.

---

Each domain teaches something applicable to all others. Video's content hierarchy informs any domain with structured items. E-commerce's conversion funnel applies wherever there's a purchase. News's diversity requirements matter everywhere fairness is a concern. The domain-specific knowledge is portable — the constraints are what make it interesting.
